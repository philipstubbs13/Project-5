Bio

Ever since I was young, I have always been adventurous and bold in trying something new no matter how difficult it was to learn.

As a kid, I liked challenging myself to explore new opportunities whether it be learning another language, learning how to play piano, trying out for the basketball team despite not being as tall as other players, or striving to be one of the best chess players at school. I always found different ways to solve problems whether it be figuring out how to solve a difficult problem in math class, how to be a couple moves ahead of my chess opponent, or how to be an efficient three point shooter on the basketball court.

Exploration, determination, and strategic thinking have been and still are a big part of who I am. My passion to explore new territories and figure out complex problems is what sparked my interest in coding.

As a result, I enrolled in the University of Minnesota coding boot camp in 2017. Since enrolling, my passion for learning about technologies like CSS and Javascript continues to grow. My interest in how those technologies impact the design and user experience of the web is why I'm excited to continue learning more.
