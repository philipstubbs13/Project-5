Writing Portfolio

In addition to coding, I also like to write. Over the years, I have developed a passion for writing, especially for the web. I enjoy taking complex, technical topics and breaking them down so that a non-technical person can understand them. In my writing portfolio, you’ll find a diverse selection of projects (both academic and professional).

Technical writing projects

Over the past few years, I’ve worked on and published many types of customer facing technical documents, including install guides, user guides, configuration guides, release notes, online help, and reference material. Sometimes, the writing work was more of an individual effort, and other times, the projects I worked on required me to work closely with engineers, developers, human factors, and other writers to meet the project release date.

The following samples are from several recent projects I worked on. I want to mention that I rarely worked on a document one at at time from start to finish. I often found myself having to work on many documents at once covering different topics and products. So, these samples not only represent my writing and editing skills, but they also illustrate my ability to prioritize multiple tasks and deadlines by delivering each document on time for each release in a fast-paced environment.
